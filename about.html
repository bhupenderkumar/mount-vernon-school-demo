Our Mission & History

For over 30 years, Mount Vernon School has been a trusted partner for families in Atlanta, providing a rigorous, well-rounded education that prepares students for success in college and beyond.

Our experienced faculty and staff design engaging lesson plans that cater to diverse learning styles, incorporating hands-on activities, project-based learning, and collaborative projects that foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

From sports teams to clubs, music programs to drama productions, our students have numerous opportunities to explore their passions, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships within a vibrant, inclusive community.
