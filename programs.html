Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is specifically designed for children aged 2-4, with a focus on play-based learning, socialization, and emotional development. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that encourages curiosity and exploration.

📚

Elementary

From grades K-5, our elementary program emphasizes foundational skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and science, alongside creative expression in music, art, and physical education.

📖

Middle School

In grades 6-8, our middle school program builds on the foundations established in elementary school, with a focus on developing critical thinking, research, and presentation skills, as well as exploring interests in STEM, arts, and athletics.

🎓

Upper School

From grades 9-12, our upper school program prepares students for college and beyond, with advanced courses in mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies, as well as electives in arts, humanities, and STEM fields.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program offers a range of courses and clubs that foster interest in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, from robotics and coding to physics and engineering design.

🎨

Arts

From music and theater to visual arts and dance, our arts program provides numerous opportunities for students to express themselves creatively and develop their skills in a variety of disciplines.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletics program offers a range of sports teams and clubs for students of all skill levels, from competitive teams to recreational activities, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
