Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-4, where we nurture their curiosity and creativity through play-based learning and hands-on activities.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students in grades K-5 develop a strong foundation in reading, writing, mathematics, and science, with a focus on hands-on learning and critical thinking.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students in grades 6-12, where we challenge them to excel academically, develop their passions, and prepare them for success in college and beyond.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program offers a rigorous and interdisciplinary approach to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with hands-on projects and real-world applications.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program provides students with the opportunity to explore their creativity through music, drama, visual arts, and dance, with performances and exhibitions throughout the year.

⚽️

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports teams for students in grades 6-12, where we emphasize teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness, with competitive games and tournaments throughout the year.
